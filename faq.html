Why is my AI not working?

There could be several reasons why your AI bot is not working as expected. Here are a few troubleshooting steps:

  • **Check your integration:** Ensure the AI bot is correctly integrated with your website or application. Refer to our integration guide for step-by-step instructions.
  • **Check your settings:** Review the bot's configuration to make sure all settings are correct for your specific use case.
  • **Clear your cache:** Sometimes, outdated browser or application cache can interfere with performance.
  • **Contact support:** If the issue persists, please reach out to our dedicated support team with a detailed description of the problem. We are here to help and will get your AI back on track.
